Guess The Name!

Instructions

  • Use the keyboard to guess a letter.
  • If your guess is correct, you will see the letter added to the blank spaces.
  • If your guess is incorrect, you will see your "chances" go down by one and the letter added to the "guessed letters" list.
  • You will repeat the process until you have guessed the word, or your chances reached zero.

Press Any Key To Begin!
